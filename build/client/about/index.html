David Hewitt is a Civil Engineer and licensed general contractor with experience in residential and commercial construction. David graduated from North Carolina State University's College of Engineering. While in college, he started his career in construction as a field engineer for a large construction company, later moving into a project management role. David has project management experience in several market segments, including multifamily homes, healthcare, and municipal.

As the founder of Hewitt Builders, David wants to restore confidence in the construction process and deliver high-quality projects. His strong work ethic and attention to detail, combined with a keen ability to problem-solve, enable him to set clear expectations and provide high-quality results.
